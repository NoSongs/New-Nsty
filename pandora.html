Pandora

Pandora For Artists

2013

This past year, I was invited to join Pandora’s very talented design team to work on a suite of tools to help musicians and labels understand and interact with their audience on the platform. Pandora For Artists had three major pillars for research and design: Data & Analytics, Artist Presence, and Music Submission.

My focus was on Analytics/Data Visualization for both mobile, web and artist presence. Because the final product is not yet launched, unfortunately I can’t go into further detail at this time. But let’s just leave it at this, it’s pretty freakin’ awesome.
